Site Plan for Moandemah Foundation

Site Name

Name: Moandemah Foundation

Reason:This name is in the Kono language which means 'Let's Help.' It reflects the organization's mission to empower and uplift women and girls in Sierra Leone through collective action and support..

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide a visually stunning and educational platform where users can learn about Moandemah Foundation's initiatives, access resources, and engage with the community to support women and girls in Sierra Leone.

Scenarios

  • What charity organization in my community is engaged in feminine hygiene initiatives?
  • How can I learn more about the impact of Moandemah Foundation's work on my community?
  • How can I get involved with Moandemah Foundation's initiatives?

Color Schema

#005B99
#ff6f61
#333333
#F5F5F5

Usage:

  • Primary Color - Dark Gray [#333333]: Text color
  • Secondary color - Warm coral [#F4A261]: Used for primary call-to-action buttons, borders, dividers, active Links/hover Effects.
  • Accent Color - Trustworthy Blue [#005B99]: Used for nav backgrounds and links to convey trust and reliability.
  • Background Color - Light Gray [#F5F5F5]: Background for content sections like the hero and photo containers.

Typography

  • Montserrat: Used for headers and titles to create emphasis and clarity.
  • Body Font - Cabin:Used for body text to ensure readability and modern design and simplicity.
