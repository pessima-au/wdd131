Site Plan for Moandemah Foundation

Site Name

Name: Moandemah Foundation

Reason: This is the official website for the Moandemah Foundation, a community-focused nonprofit organization in Sierra Leone dedicated to empowering children, families, and vulnerable communities. The name clearly matches the identity and mission of the foundation.

Logo

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide an accessible, trustworthy, and informative platform where donors, partners, volunteers, and community members can learn about the foundation’s mission, explore ongoing projects, contribute through donations, and contact the organization easily.

It will also serve as a transparency and communication tool by showcasing completed work, annual reports, and impact stories.

Scenarios

  • How can I donate to support Moandemah Foundation’s education or health projects?
  • Where is the organization located, and how can I contact or volunteer?
  • What current projects are being implemented, and how can my organization partner?

Color Scheme

#3a86ff
#ec9619
#000000

Usage:

  • #3a86ff (Blue): Primary brand color—used for headers, buttons, and call-to-action areas.
  • #ec9619 (Orange): Secondary color—used for success messages, donation sections, and accents.
  • #000000 (Black): Clean text color for readability.

Typography

  • Montserrat: Used for headers, titles, and emphasis text.
  • Cabin: Used for body text for improved readability and clean presentation.
