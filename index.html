About Me

My name is Albert Pessima. I was born in Rotifunk, Southern Sierra Leone. I currently live with my family in Freetown, Sierra Leone. I work as a project manager at a construction company. I have a passion for computers, which has led me to study software development. I enjoy spending time at the beach, especially with my family, and also watching a good game of football. I am currently pursuing a degree in software development at BYU-Idaho to expand my knowledge and skills in web technologies. I am particularly interested in building responsive and user-friendly web applications. In my free time, I like reading about new tech trends, learning new programming languages, and collaborating on open-source projects. I value teamwork, continuous learning, and making a positive impact in my community through technology.

Freetown, Sierra Leone

Sierra Leone is a small country in West African known for its rich natural resources, vibrant culture, and recent history of resilience. The population of Sierra Leone is around 8 million, with Freetown as its capital and largest city. The country is bordered by Guinea to the north and east, Liberia to the southeast, and the Atlantic Ocean to the southwest, giving it beautiful coastlines and tropical weather. Freetown is the capital and largest city of Sierra Leone, located on the Atlantic coast. It is known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant culture, and historical significance.
